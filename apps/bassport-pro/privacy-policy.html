Privacy Policy - Bassport

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Bassport is a car-audio utility app. This Privacy Policy explains what data may be processed and how it is used.

What Data Is Collected

  • Basic analytics events (screen visits, feature usage, app version).
  • Crash and diagnostics information for reliability and bug fixing.
  • Advertising identifiers and ad interaction data when ads are shown.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Analytics for product analytics.
  • Google AdMob and/or Appodeal for ad delivery and measurement.

Data Storage

Analytics and ad-related data are processed by service providers under their own retention policies. The app does not require account registration.

Children's Data (COPPA)

Bassport is not directed to children under 13. If you believe child data was submitted by mistake, contact us and we will assist.

Your Rights

You may request information, correction, or deletion by contacting us via email.
