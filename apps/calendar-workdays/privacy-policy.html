Privacy Policy - Calendar Workdays

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Calendar Workdays helps users plan shifts and track work schedules. This page describes privacy and data usage.

What Data Is Collected

  • Schedule usage analytics and app interaction events.
  • Crash diagnostics for reliability.
  • Advertising identifiers and ad interaction data.
  • Purchase status metadata handled via in-app purchase providers.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Analytics for product analytics.
  • Google AdMob and/or Appodeal for advertising.
  • RevenueCat for subscription and in-app purchase management.

Data Storage

Some data is processed by third-party services according to their policies. App account creation is not required.

Children's Data (COPPA)

The app is not intended for children under 13. Contact us if you believe a child provided personal data.

Your Rights

You can request details, correction, or deletion by writing to support email.
