Privacy Policy - Calendar Workdays

Last updated: February 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Calendar Workdays ("the App") may collect, use, and protect information when you use the App. By using Calendar Workdays, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

  • Usage data (screens, actions, app version, and feature events).
  • Crash logs and diagnostics to improve app stability.
  • Advertising identifiers and ad interaction data when ads are shown.
  • Purchase and subscription status metadata processed via in-app purchase infrastructure.

How We Use Information

  • To provide scheduling, shift tracking, and calendar functionality.
  • To monitor reliability, fix errors, and improve product quality.

Third-Party Services

Calendar Workdays may use the following third-party services:

These providers process data under their own privacy policies and retention practices.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared with service providers only for app operation, analytics, diagnostics, subscriptions, and advertising, or when required by law.

Data handled by third-party services may be retained according to their policies. We apply reasonable safeguards, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

Calendar Workdays is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data through the App, please contact us.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of certain data. Contact us to submit a request.
