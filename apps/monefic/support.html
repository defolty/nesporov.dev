FAQ

Where is my finance data stored?
Monefic stores data on your device by default. Optional iCloud sync can be enabled from the Account screen.
Can I work with multiple currencies?
Yes. Monefic supports multiple currencies and lets you set conversion rates for combined statistics.
Can I export my data?
Yes. You can export selected records as a readable text report, CSV, or JSON.
Does Monefic work without iCloud?
Yes. Core tracking, planning, statistics, import, and export features work with local data.
How can I report a bug?
Email the device model, iOS version, app version, and steps that reproduce the issue. Please remove sensitive financial information from screenshots or attachments.
