FAQ

Does SlowDown need Apple Health access?
Yes, the app may request motion and health permissions to monitor pace-related metrics.
Is my data synced to a server?
Current core pace processing is designed to stay on-device.
Why am I not feeling haptic feedback?
Check Apple Watch haptics settings and confirm workout/session mode is active.
How can I report an issue?
Send Apple Watch model, watchOS version, and issue details by email.
