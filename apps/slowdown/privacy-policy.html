Privacy Policy - SlowDown

Last updated: February 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SlowDown ("the App") may collect, use, and protect information when you use the App. By using SlowDown, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

  • Health and motion-related data required to provide pacing and haptic guidance features (subject to your permissions).
  • Basic diagnostics and crash information to improve app stability.

How We Use Information

  • To provide walking pace feedback and haptic guidance features.
  • To improve reliability, performance, and user experience.

Third-Party Services

SlowDown does not currently use advertising SDKs and does not use ad identifiers. If third-party services are added in future releases, this policy will be updated.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. Information may be disclosed only when required by law or to protect rights, safety, or integrity of the service.

Data Retention and Security

Core pace-related processing is designed to work on-device. We apply reasonable safeguards, but no transmission or storage method is completely secure.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

SlowDown is not intended for children under 13 without supervision. If you believe a child has provided data through the App, please contact us.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request information, correction, deletion, or restriction related to processed data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact
