Privacy Policy - SlowDown

Last updated: February 25, 2026

SlowDown is a watchOS app for pacing support through haptic feedback.

What Data Is Collected

  • Health and activity metrics required for walking pace feedback.
  • Basic diagnostics for crash investigation and stability improvements.

Data Processing

SlowDown does not include advertising SDKs and does not use ad identifiers.

Third-Party Services

No third-party ad services are used in the current release.

Data Storage

Core activity data is processed on-device to provide pacing functionality.

Children's Data (COPPA)

SlowDown is not intended for children under 13 without parent supervision.

Your Rights

You may contact us to request clarification about data processing.
