Privacy Policy - Nesporov Apps

Last updated: February 25, 2026

This page summarizes privacy principles used across apps published by Nikita Nesporov. App-specific details are available on each app page in /apps/<app-id>/privacy-policy.html.

Data Collection

Depending on the app, data may include diagnostics, analytics events, and ad identifiers to measure performance and ad relevance.

Third-Party Services

Apps can use Firebase Analytics, Google AdMob, Appodeal, and RevenueCat based on product functionality.

User Rights and Contact

You can request deletion or clarification by contacting main@nesporov.dev.
